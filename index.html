Quick tips to keep charts moving fast:

Drag people from Team Library onto the canvas, then connect cards or let auto-connectors place rows for you.

Style

Use the Canvas Controls panel for layout, colors, card styles, and exports.

Save

Start a new chart from New Canvas, then save it into the Chart Library for reuse.

Shortcuts

Undo, redo, fit all, mini map, zoom, and export are all up in the top toolbar.
